Privacy Policy for AutoEmailBot

AutoEmailBot respects the privacy of users and protects their personal information. This page explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Information We Access

  • The app only accesses your Gmail inbox to read and send automated emails.
  • No data is stored on our servers. Everything happens temporarily and only for sending emails.
  • No data is shared with any other person or any third-party service.

How Data is Used

  • Data is used only to generate automated responses and send emails.
  • The app uses OpenAI to create email text. The content is not saved permanently.
  • No information is transferred outside the Google servers except for requested email generation.

Security

We do not store, share, or sell your data. The app only acts based on the permissions you grant through Google OAuth.
